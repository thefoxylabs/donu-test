Donu's World

A personal glimpse into Donu's thoughts, travels and experiences.

Welcome to Donu's World: Wit, Wisdom, and a Pinch of Pepper!

Step into the world of Donu Kogbara—a seasoned journalist, media commentator, and unapologetically authentic storyteller. Through her lens, readers are invited on a personal journey that stretches across Nigeria and beyond, infused with sharp wit, heartfelt insights, and moments of unfiltered realness.

Donu’s work has been featured in leading publications like Vanguard and The Africa Report, and she has appeared on global media platforms including Channel 4, BBC, Arise, Al Jazeera, and Sky. When she’s not delivering sharp, thoughtful commentary, she’s facilitating powerful conversations, bridging cultural gaps, and offering strategic counsel to decision-makers. Yet, there’s much more to her world than headlines and high-level meetings.

Donu’s World is a vibrant blend of life’s highs and lows—where the challenges of single parenthood, the pursuit of wellness, and the hilarity of everyday moments take center stage. It’s an honest and often humorous account of trying (and failing, then trying again), all served with a hearty dose of Nigerian flavour.

And the journey doesn’t end with her. Donu opens the floor for others to share their own stories—because here, every voice matters.
