About Donu

Donu Kogbara is a seasoned print/broadcast journalist and communications expert. She grew up in the UK, studied Combined Arts at the University of Leicester and started her career in London, where she worked for well-known media outlets such as the Economist Intelligence Unit, Sunday Times newspaper, High Life (British Airways in-flight magazine) and the BBC.
